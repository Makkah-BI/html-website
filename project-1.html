Our story

The Problem

Local flower shops have no online presence. Customers can't find them, see their work, or know their story. They lose business to big online retailers.

My Approach

I built a clean semantic site with header, hero, about, bouquet gallery, and contact form. I used article tags for each bouquet. I added anchor navigation.

Hardest Lesson

Learning semantic tags (article vs section) was confusing. Also realized forms need backend, they look real but don't send emails yet.
