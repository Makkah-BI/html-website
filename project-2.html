The Problem

Small Kenyan NGOs lack visibility and donor trust. Without websites, they can't attract funding or volunteers to help children in need.

My Approach

I built a transparent site with mission, impact stats, programs and donation table showing exactly what money buys ($10 = 30 meals). I added volunteer and donate forms.

Hardest Lesson

The forms are complex, donation form needed radio buttons, dropdowns and card fields. I researched real costs in Kenya to make it believable.
